How It Works

1

Add Bot to Group

Add the bot to your Telegram group and make it an admin with necessary permissions.

2

New Member Joins

When a new member joins, they're automatically muted and prompted to verify.

3

Face Verification

User scans QR code or taps button, completes face verification in Mercle app.

4

Instant Access

Upon successful verification, user is unmuted and can participate in the group.
